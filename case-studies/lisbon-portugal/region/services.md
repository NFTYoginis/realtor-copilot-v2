# Local Services — Lisbon, Portugal (Case Study)

**Illustrative example entries.** Real Lisbon agents should replace with their actual vendor list. The format and the *kinds of notes worth keeping* (specialty, language coverage, foreign-buyer fluency) are the transferable lesson.

Lisbon is a heavily international market. Vendors who can operate in English, French, and ideally Brazilian Portuguese have outsized value. Specialty matters too: heritage-building inspectors are different from modern-stock inspectors; AL-savvy attorneys are different from generalists.
