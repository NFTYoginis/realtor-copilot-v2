# Region — Glossary

Novato is an English-monolingual market. Plain-English vocabulary covers most agent work.

## California-specific technical terms (worth noting for out-of-state buyers and agents)

| Term | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|
| Prop 13 | California property tax limit | 1978 law limiting property tax to ~1% of assessed value; reassessed at sale |
| Mello-Roos | Special tax assessment district | Annual tax in newer subdivisions to fund infrastructure; not in all neighborhoods |
| TDS | Transfer Disclosure Statement | Required CA seller disclosure of property condition |
| NHD | Natural Hazard Disclosure | Required CA disclosure of flood, fire, earthquake, environmental hazards |
| FIRPTA | Foreign Investment in Real Property Tax Act | Federal withholding on foreign sellers (15% of sale price) |
| HOA | Homeowners Association | Applies to some planned communities; common in Hamilton-area developments |
| 1031 exchange | Federal tax-deferred exchange | Investment-property tax-deferral mechanism |
| AB 1482 | California Rent Cap law | Annual rent increase limits for non-exempt rentals |
| SMART | Sonoma–Marin Area Rail Transit | Commuter rail; relevant for commute-fit assessment |

For most listing and matching tasks, no glossary lookup is needed — the specialist's plain-English vocabulary suffices.
