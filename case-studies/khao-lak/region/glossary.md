---

Khao Lak is bilingual-plus: legal and Land Office documents are in Thai; foreign-buyer marketing is primarily in English; German, Russian, and Scandinavian languages appear in marketing materials. The Thai terms below appear in legal documents, contracts, and conversations with the Land Office, lawyers, and Thai sellers.

## Ownership & title

| Term | Transliteration | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| โฉนดที่ดิน | Chanote (Chanod) / Nor Sor 4 Jor | Full freehold land title | The strongest Thai land title. GPS-surveyed boundaries. The standard for transactable real estate. |
| น.ส. 3 ก. | Nor Sor Sam Gor | Confirmed land-use right | Aerial-survey boundaries; transactable but lower-grade than Chanote. Can be upgraded. |
| น.ส. 3 | Nor Sor Sam | Possession right | Lower grade still; no precise boundaries. Caution. |
| ส.ค. 1 | Sor Kor Neung | Tax-receipt-level claim | Not real title. Avoid. |
| ภ.บ.ท. 5 | Por Bor Tor Ha | Land tax payment receipt | Not title. Often misrepresented as such. Avoid. |
| ทะเบียนบ้าน | Tabien Baan | House Registration Book | Records who is registered as living at the address. Yellow Book (เล่มเหลือง) version available for foreigners. |
| กรรมสิทธิ์ | Kammasit | Ownership / freehold | Generic term for ownership right. |
| สิทธิการเช่า | Sit Karn Chao | Leasehold right | The right under a registered lease. |
| นิติบุคคลอาคารชุด | Niti Bukkhon Akhan Chud | Condominium juristic person | The legal entity managing a condo. Issues the foreign-quota letter and common-fee certificate. |

## Tax & fees

| Term | Transliteration | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| ค่าธรรมเนียมการโอน | Kha Thamniam Karn Oan | Transfer fee | 2% of appraised value at Land Office; commonly split 50/50 between buyer and seller. |
| ภาษีธุรกิจเฉพาะ | Phasi Thurakit Chapho | Specific Business Tax (SBT) | 3.3% on sale price or appraised value; applies if seller held <5 years and not primary residence. |
| อากรแสตมป์ | Akon Stamp | Stamp Duty | 0.5% of sale price; applies when SBT does not. |
| ภาษีหัก ณ ที่จ่าย | Phasi Hak Na Thi Jai | Withholding Tax | Withheld at Land Office on appraised value; progressive for individuals, flat 1% for company sellers. |
| ภาษีที่ดินและสิ่งปลูกสร้าง | Phasi Thi Din Lae Sing Pluk Sang | Land and Building Tax | Annual property tax (replaced older House and Land Tax in 2020). |
| ค่าส่วนกลาง | Kha Suan Klang | Common-area fee | Monthly/annual fee paid to the condo juristic person. |
| ค่าจดทะเบียน | Kha Jot Thabian | Registration fee | Land Office registration cost (transfer fee, mortgage registration, lease registration). |

## Building / property type

| Term | Transliteration | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| คอนโดมิเนียม / คอนโด | Condominium / Condo | Condominium | Apartment in a registered condominium building. The only residential unit type a foreigner can own freehold. |
| อพาร์ตเมนต์ | Apartment | Apartment | Rental apartment (NOT a condo — important distinction; "apartment" buildings cannot sell freehold to foreigners). |
| บ้านเดี่ยว | Baan Diao | Detached house / villa | Single-family detached home. |
| พูลวิลล่า | Pool Villa | Pool villa | The dominant marketed product in Khao Lak — detached villa with private pool. |
| ทาวน์เฮ้าส์ | Town House | Townhouse | Attached row house. |
| ตึกแถว / อาคารพาณิชย์ | Tuek Thaeo / Akhan Phanit | Shophouse / commercial building | Mixed commercial-residential row building. |
| ที่ดินเปล่า | Thi Din Plao | Vacant land | Bare land plot. |
| ไร่ | Rai | Rai (land area) | Thai land unit. **1 rai = 1,600 m² = 4 ngan = 400 wah**. Standard unit for land pricing. |
| งาน | Ngan | Ngan (land area) | **1 ngan = 400 m² = 100 wah**. Quarter of a rai. |
| ตารางวา | Tarang Wah | Square wah | **1 wah = 4 m²**. Standard unit for plot dimensions. |
| ตารางเมตร / ตร.ม. | Tarang Met | Square meter | Standard unit for built area. |

## Transaction

| Term | Transliteration | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| สำนักงานที่ดิน | Samnak Ngan Thi Din | Land Office | Government office where transfers, leases, and mortgages are registered. Khao Lak transactions go through Takua Pa District Land Office. |
| สัญญาจะซื้อจะขาย | Sanya Ja Sue Ja Khai | Sale and Purchase Agreement (SPA) | The binding sale contract signed before transfer. |
| สัญญาเช่า | Sanya Chao | Lease agreement | Lease contract; must be registered at Land Office if >3 years. |
| มัดจำ | Mat Jam | Reservation deposit / earnest money | Deposit paid at LOI / reservation. |
| โอนกรรมสิทธิ์ | Oan Kammasit | Transfer of ownership | The Land Office transfer event. |
| FET / Tor Tor 3 | (English / ธ.ต. 3) | Foreign Exchange Transaction form | Bank-issued form documenting inbound foreign-currency remittance. **Critical document** — required for foreign condo freehold registration AND for repatriating sale proceeds. |
| ใบอนุญาตก่อสร้าง | Bai Anuyat Kor Sang | Construction permit | Building permit. |
| หนังสือมอบอำนาจ | Nangsue Mob Amnaj | Power of attorney | POA on prescribed Land Office form. |
| โอนเงินจากต่างประเทศ | Oan Ngern Jak Tang Prathet | International remittance | Inbound funds from abroad. |
| ผังเมือง | Phang Mueang | Town/zoning plan | Local zoning controls (height limits, use class). Coastal Khao Lak is subject to specific height and setback rules. |
