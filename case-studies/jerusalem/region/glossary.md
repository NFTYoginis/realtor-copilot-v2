# Region — Glossary

Key Hebrew real estate terms used by Jerusalem agents, with transliteration and English equivalents.

---

## Ownership & title

| Term | Transliteration | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| טאבו | Tabu | Land Registry | Full title registration; the gold-standard ownership record |
| חברה משכנת | Khevra Meshakenet | Housing company registration | Older form; title held by a housing company; common in pre-1990s buildings |
| גוש / חלקה / תת חלקה | Gush / Helka / Tat-Helka | Block / parcel / sub-parcel | Israeli land identification system |
| טופס 4 | Tofes 4 | Form 4 | Occupancy permit; required for legal transfer |

## Tax & fees

| Term | Transliteration | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| מס רכישה | Mas Rechisha | Purchase tax | Tax on real estate purchase, paid by buyer |
| מס שבח | Mas Shevach | Capital gains tax | Tax on real estate appreciation, paid by seller |
| ארנונה | Arnona | Municipal property tax | Annual local tax based on zone and area |
| ועד בית | Vaad Bayit | Building committee | Monthly fee for shared building maintenance |
| היטל השבחה | Heitel Hashbacha | Improvement tax | Tax owed when zoning changes increase property value |

## Building / property type

| Term | Transliteration | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| ממ״ד | Mamad | Reinforced security room | Required in post-1992 construction |
| דירת גן | Dirat Gan | Garden apartment | Ground-floor unit with private garden |
| פנטהאוז | Penthouse | Penthouse | Top-floor unit; in Israel often includes private terrace |
| דופלקס | Dupleks | Duplex | Two-story unit (often within an apartment building) |
| דירת גג | Dirat Gag | Roof apartment | Top-floor unit; sometimes synonymous with penthouse |
| תמ״א 38 | Tama 38 | NMP 38 (earthquake retrofit) | National plan; can affect buying decisions |

## Transaction

| Term | Transliteration | English equivalent | Brief definition |
|---|---|---|---|
| מתווך | Metavech | Real estate agent | Licensed agent |
| שמאי | Shamai | Appraiser | Licensed property appraiser |
| מפונה / תפוס | Mefonneh / Tafus | Vacated / Occupied | Property status — vacant vs. tenant in place |
| נדל״ן | Nadlan | Real estate | Industry shorthand |
| ייפוי כוח | Yipui Koach | Power of attorney | Required for many remote-close foreign-buyer transactions |
